CLTV Prediction Based on Customer Information

Mentor: Prof. HF Ting

Understanding future customer behavior and making predictions for a customer at an individual level is essential for most companies. It is beneficial for companies to accurately quantize customer behavior as values by mathematical modeling in order to propose a significant customer relationship management strategy. Researchers have developed various customer lifetime value (CLTV) systems in different fields of industries such as the retail industry, E-commerce, game industry, and so on. Our project aims to construct a deep-learning-based CLTV prediction system that can accurately mine the potential relationship between customer information and CLTV. The system can utilize predicted CLTV to visualize potentially valuable information on a dashboard, which is helpful for companies in analyzing future customer behavior and producing a significant customer relationship management strategy.
